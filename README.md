# DSA

### tips to follow

- solve each problem on your own for at least 15 minutes. if stuck, check the solution and revisit after one week.
- think out loud or explain your reasoning for every line of code.
- revisit solved problems after a few days to reinforce learning.
- don’t just copy-paste solutions, understand the logic behind them.
- solve problems using different approaches, from brute force to optimized.
- focus on time and space complexity.
- solve dsa problems daily for consistency and progress.
